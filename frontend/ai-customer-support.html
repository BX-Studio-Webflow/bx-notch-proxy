AGENTIC TICKET RESOLUTION OVERVIEW

How Notch Resolves Support Tickets

Instantly identifies the account, pinpoints the issue, and applies the best-fit policy, while ruling out the rest.

Every decision is backed by clear reasoning in QA mode, ensuring accurate resolutions.

Once a policy is locked in, every requirement is gathered automatically — sourced from the conversation, account, or targeted follow-ups.

Each decision is explainable and mapped to your solution & policy business rules, ensuring precise, governed resolution.

Once every requirement is met, the best solution is selected automatically.

Each step, from fulfillment checks to back-office actions, is executed through API integrations or flagged for a human agent.

Behind every ticket, parallel workflows automatically retrieve data, enforce policies, and coordinate tasks.

Notch applies deterministic rules to deliver the precision and consistency your CS team needs.
